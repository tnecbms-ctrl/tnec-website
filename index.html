About Us

An Emerging Force in Infrastructure Consultancy

Established in 2022, True North Engineering Consultants LLP is a rapidly growing organization committed to excellence in Topographical Survey, GIS, Remote Sensing, Bathymetry, Water Supply and Sewerage projects.

Our continuing objective is to deliver all services with keen observation — maintaining efficiency, accuracy and quality. Our founding directors bring 10+ years of hands-on experience since 2015, having delivered 100+ projects through their prior venture before establishing True North Engineering Consultants LLP in 2022.
