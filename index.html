About Us

An Emerging Force in Infrastructure Consultancy

Established in 2022, True North Engineering Consultants LLP is a rapidly growing organization with a goal of achieving the best results in the industry. We bring excellence-level knowledge and practical experience in Topographical Survey, GIS, Remote Sensing, Bathymetry, Water Supply and Sewerage projects.

Our continuing objective is to provide all services with keen observation — maintaining efficiency, accuracy and quality at their best. We've covered districts across Madhya Pradesh and multiple states, delivering full engineering consultancy for Water Supply and Sewerage Schemes. We have completed more than 15,000 KM of Survey work.

What We Do Best

Topographical Survey
Total Station and DGPS survey, profile & contour mapping, earthwork calculation for roads, layouts and infrastructure corridors.
03

Water Supply Design

04

Bathymetry Survey
River contouring, dam capacity analysis, contour interval surveys using echo-sounder for water resource management.
05
🏗️
Sewer Network Design
Sewage network survey, LWM hydraulic design, STP sizing, IPS, drain survey and flow accumulation analysis for towns.
06
🚁

Aerial drone surveys, photogrammetry, ground penetrating radar (GPR) and digital terrain model generation for urban projects.
07
📋
DPR Consultancy
Detailed project report preparation for water supply, sewerage, waterbody rejuvenation, green space and LWM under AMRUT 2.0.
08

Door-to-door household and asset GIS survey with prescribed questionnaire formats using digital survey methodology.
